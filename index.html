Creativity, science and a holiday under the pines

A Place of Power: Luukki Estate

Our residence sits on a comfortable base in the lake district of Espoo, next to the historic Luukki Estate. The drive from Helsinki takes only about 40 minutes. It's a true nature reserve, where modern, comfortable facilities blend harmoniously into a landscape of century-old pines and a pristine lake.

Here the spirit of relaxed countryside intellectual life meets modern technology. Safe piers by the water are set up for rest and taking in nature. The grounds feature well-kept lawns and sports areas for active time outdoors.

Programs by Age

We bring together children from different countries and cities, forming a warm community. So everyone can develop at their own pace and find like-minded peers, we divide participants into age groups.

Kids 🧩
Ages 10 — 14.5

The age of discovery and experiment. Through play and hands-on practice, we get to know technology and visual art and build our first serious projects with our own hands.

Teens ⚡
Ages 14.5 — 17

Grown-up conversation, complex concepts and informed choices. The program is built on project-lab principles: prototyping, discussion, and the search for professional direction.

Daily Life, Comfort and Safety

🥗 Food

Five healthy meals a day: a buffet plus snacks. The buffet always offers fresh croissants, hot dishes (such as syrniki), boiled eggs, and healthy extras. There's plenty of vegetables and protein, even for vegans. Special diets are possible: allergen-free meals and accommodation for any restriction.

🛡️ Safety and Care

We provide the highest level of attention: the adult-to-child ratio on each session is 1:3. A counseling psychologist and a medic are present at all times during every session.

🔥 Atmosphere

In the evenings we gather around a safe, beautifully built campfire. It's a time for songs with a guitar, warm conversations, and strengthening friendships in our little village.

🚌 Transfer

Group airport transfer is arranged on comfortable buses (€30 each way).

Immersion: Meetings and Excursions

Our holiday is a chance to see how real professionals work. We organise unique visits and meetings.

🎮 Game Dev: SuperCell

We'll visit the headquarters of the legendary Finnish studio SuperCell. Participants will meet a product manager who is currently working on the company's new game.

🔬 Science: Aalto University

We'll head to the cutting-edge Aalto University to see the latest inventions of its students and researchers firsthand.

⚡ Transport of the Future

Planned: a meeting with Anton Poppel — inventor and founder of the Solar Regatta project and of companies in micromobility and self-driving transport. The kids will get to test and build remarkable machines themselves, including custom electric karts.

🤖 Artificial Intelligence

A meeting with a founder of DeepPavlov.ai — a foundational voice-recognition model built at MIPT, whose technology now powers Yandex, Sber, and other large systems.
